Diving with LUSAC

Our club is organised by divers, for divers. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced technical diver, we provide a safe and social environment to progress your skills.

We regularly dive in local waters using our well-maintained club gear available to all members. Check the calendar to see our upcoming pool sessions and coastal trips.
